Welcome to NeuroTechSC

We are an undergraduate student Neurotechnology club at the University of California at Santa Cruz. This fall quarter, we are holding general body meetings to teach concepts in NeuroTech such as hardware tools like EEG and data analysis algorithms like CSP. We are also currently building a project to submit to NeuroTechX's global competition. For more information, check out NeuroTechX.
