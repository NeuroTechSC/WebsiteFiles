Club Overview

We are NeuroTechSC, the UC Santa Cruz Chapter of NeuroTechX! We focus on project building and education covering the topics of Brain-Computer Interaction, Neuroscience, and Artficial Intelligence. We have four teams which focus on hardware, machine learning, data, and software respectively.

We received 1st place in the U.S. in the NeuroTechX Student Competition for a silent-speech interface that takes in user EMG signals from the jawline area and predicts if a user is thinking ‘yes’ or ‘no’ with 90% accuracy. We welcome you to connect with us at our weekly meetings on Tuesdays at 7pm.

Teams

Hardware

The Hardware Team is responsible for recording brain activity and developing systems to do so. The Hardware Team uses an 8-channel OpenBCI Cyton board .

Data

The Data Team is responsible for cleaning the noisy brain activity data into a clean and interpretable format. The Data Team uses a variety of techniques including Fast-Fourier Transforms and data normalization.

Machine Learning

Machine Learning is utilized to interpret and classify cleaned data into associated actions. The Machine Learning team trains our cleaned data on state-of-the-art Deep Learning Models.

Software

The Software Team bridges the work of the Hardware, Data, and Machine Learning teams. Our software's pipeline records user data, cleans it, and passes it through our trained Deep Learning model.
